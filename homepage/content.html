Journey and Explore

Explore Japan

Spring is one of the best times to visit Japan. The cherry blossom season highlights the country's natural beauty, turning sightseeing spots like parks and temples even more picturesque during this time of year. Picnic under the cherry blossom trees, attend spring festivals, and view the traditional art of ikebana (flower arrangement). The weather during this season is mild and comfortable, perfect for outdoor activities.

Explore South Korea

Visit South Korea in spring. Whether it be in the bustling core of Seoul or out in the countryside, experience vibrant festivities such as the Jinhae Cherry Blossom Festival and the Gyeongju Cherry Blossom Marathon. Indulge in traditional Korean cuisine: Jjukkumi (spicy grilled baby octopus), or Chimaek (fried chicken and beer) while enjoying the view at the Han river.

Explore Taiwan

Take in the beautiful natural landscapes in Taiwan in spring. The island is known for its cherry blossoms, which bloom in abundance during this season. Participate in kite-flying festivals and outdoor concerts, and experience Taiwan's rich culture and heritage with traditional festivals like the Tomb Sweeping Day and the Lantern Festival. The warm climate makes for an exceptional experience while taking in Taiwan's natural beauty, including its national parks and coastal scenery.
